Classic Models Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to explore sales performance, customer trends, product categories and revenue drivers, using a clean star schema and DAX measures.

Problem

Create a business-ready reporting dashboard that helps stakeholders answer questions such as: Which products drive revenue? Which customers and regions perform best? How do sales trends change over time?

Data

  • Dataset: Classic Models (sales, orders, customers, products)
  • Entities: customers, orders, order details, products, employees, offices (as applicable)
  • Preparation: data cleaning, relationship mapping, data types, date table creation (if used)

Methods

  • Data model: star schema approach for reliable analytics
  • DAX: measures for revenue, profit (if applicable), YoY/period comparisons, KPIs
  • Visuals: KPI cards, trend lines, category breakdowns, customer ranking, slicers for exploration
  • UX: clear layout, filters for product line, region, date, customer segments (as applicable)

Results

  • Delivered a dashboard that supports quick performance monitoring and drill-down analysis
  • Enabled identification of top customers/products and sales trends over time
  • Provided consistent KPIs through a structured model and reusable measures
